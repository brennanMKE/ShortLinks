## Description

Write `docs/analytics.md` documenting click recording and the metrics surfaced on the dashboard.

## Scope

- The click recorder: what is captured per click (`clicks` table) and when.
- The stats queries in `internal/clicks/stats.go`: `UTMStatsForLink` (per-UTM breakdown) and `ClicksOverTime` (daily buckets, the half-open 30-day default window, UTC bucketing) — see #0049.
- How the SPA consumes them: the inline-SVG charts on `LinkDetail` (`ClicksChart`, `UTMBarChart`) and the data-shaping helpers in `web/src/lib/charts.ts`.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] `docs/analytics.md` covers click recording, both stats queries (incl. bucketing assumptions), and the chart consumption
- [ ] Accurate to `internal/clicks` + the web charts
