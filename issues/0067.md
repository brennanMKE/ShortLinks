## Description

Write `docs/passkeys.md` documenting the passkey (WebAuthn) ceremonies — registration, login, and account recovery — and the credential model.

## Scope

- The three ceremonies (begin/finish endpoints + the client `navigator.credentials.create()/get()` calls in `web/src/lib/webauthn.ts`): registration, login, and recovery/lost-passkey.
- Relying Party config: `WEBAUTHN_RP_ID` / `WEBAUTHN_RP_ORIGIN` and why HTTPS + exact origin matter.
- The credential model (`passkey_credentials`), including backup-eligibility/backup-state persistence and why it matters for iCloud Keychain passkeys (see #0047).
- The first-admin enrollment path via **Recover account** (seed creates the user but no passkey; recovery enrolls one) — cross-link `DEPLOYMENT.md` step 10.
- Common failure modes and the generic `ErrLoginFailed` / server-side `login:` logging (see #0047).
